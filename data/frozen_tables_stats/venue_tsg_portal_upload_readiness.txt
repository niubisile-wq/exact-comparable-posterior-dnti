Step 2. Generate and build non-anonymous files

    powershell -NoProfile -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\build_tsg_submission_package.ps1

    manuscript_tsg_submission.pdf
    supplementary_material_tsg_submission.tex

Step 3. Re-run gate checks
  Required checks:
    Main PDF <= 10 pages.
    Logs have no LaTeX Warning, undefined references/citations, Overfull,
    Error, Emergency, or Fatal error.
    Main and supplement have real author block.
    Acknowledgment has real funding/no-funding statement and preserves AI
    disclosure.
    Data/code availability and conflict/declaration statements are present.

Step 4. Advisor review
  Required:
    Call advisor on the non-anonymous TSG package.
  Rule:
    If advisor is not PASS, repair and rerun.
    Do not proceed to portal upload before PASS.
